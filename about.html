The center is licensed by the Ohio Department of Jobs and Family Services, and is approved to operate as an infant, toddler, preschool, and school-age center. The license is posted in the office for anyone who wishes to review the conditions and authorized capacity. Kings and Queens was established to provide quality and loving care for all children ages 6 weeks to 13 years of age. We believe that, with the right foundation, anything is possible! We take pride in helping to build active minds and healthy bodies. We are passionate about delivering our promise to families to be your trusted partner and to provide the best early childhood education for your child. Our staff recognizes the importance of balanced growth, providing opportunities for mental, physical, and emotional growth through a variety of creative experiences.
